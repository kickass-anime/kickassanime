Ling Yao – “Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood”

Prince of the Xing Empire (correspondingly Chinese in the world of " Fullmetal Alchemist"), Ling Yao arrives in Amestris to find the mythical Prophet Stone. He has superhuman martial arts, which is comparable to those who cultivate metal bending with supernatural powers.

Vegeta – “Dragon Ball”

The son of the late King Vegeta, Prince Vegeta, was the last surviving member of the royal family and one of the last few Saiyans in the " Dragon Ball" universe. Despite losing his home planet, he still proudly calls himself the " Prince of the Saiyan People" and one of the series' proudest warriors.

Prince Endymion (Chiba Mamoru) – "Sailor Moon"

Once upon a time, Prince Endymion fell in love with Princess Serenity of the Kingdom of the Moon, and when Queen Beryl and her evil army attacked the Kingdom of the Moon, Endymion came to the reinforcements, fought, and died heroically. Later, he was reincarnated as Chiba Mamoru on Earth, while Princess Serenity became Tsukino Usagi.

Ashitaka – "Princess Mononoke"

Ashitaka is the prince of Emishi, a fictional Japanese in the Ghibli anime world. He was a good marksman and very brave, always defending his homeland against all evil forces, but then one day he was hit by a curse of evil. The young prince must go into exile everywhere to find a cure before the curse spreads to his whole body.

Zen Wistalia Clarines – “Snow White with the Red Hair”

As the second prince of the Kingdom of Clarines, Zen is the perfect role model for becoming a fairytale prince. He's funny, humble, rich, and even dapper, plus his willingness to help others in times of need, or to care about his country.

Zechs Marquise (Millardo Peacecraft) – “Mobile Suit Gundam Wing”

Born prince Milliardo Peacecraft of the Kingdom of Sanc, he soon witnessed his homeland being destroyed by the good military forces of the Earth Alliance. Wearing a hat over his face and taking on a new identity, he becomes Zechs Marquise and joins the secret organization Oz to overthrow the Union.

Arslan – “The Heroic Legend of Arslan”

Prince of the Kingdom of Pars, Arslan was forced to flee his homeland at an early age by invaders. Often underestimated by his enemies for his small and gentle physique, Arslan is a brilliant warrior, combined with the leading nature of a prince and intelligent intelligence. But his strongest advantage is his loyalty and devotion to his friends around him, regardless of their inferior status.

Cecil Aijima – “Uta no Prince Sama”

Prince of Annapolis, Cecil must live with the curse of transforming him into a black cat. This evil curse can only be broken by true love, whether it can be temporarily neutralized by a kiss or magical ability. A kind and sweet person, Cecil loved Haruka at first sight and always tried to help her, even in cat form.

Soma Asman Kadar – “Black Butler”

Soma was the 26th son of the King of Bengal and lived a regal life in a palace full of servants. But when his friend and maid Meena suddenly disappear, he leaves for England with his housekeeper Agni to search for her. Although Soma is a bit spoiled as a prince accustomed to living in velvet, he is still a good and honest man.
